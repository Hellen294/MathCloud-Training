Introduction

This unit focuses on leveraging Gmail and Google Chat to improve communication efficiency within the classroom. Effective communication is key to managing a productive learning environment, ensuring that all stakeholders are informed and engaged.

Using Gmail

Setting Up Your Gmail Account

Steps:

Go to Gmail.

Sign in with your Google account.

Customize your inbox settings by clicking on the gear icon (Settings) and selecting “See all settings”.

Purpose: Tailors your Gmail experience to meet your specific needs, ensuring that your inbox is organized and functional.

Organizing Your Inbox with Labels and Filters

Steps:

Create labels to categorize emails by clicking on “More” > “Create new label”.

Set up filters by clicking on the gear icon (Settings) > “Filters and Blocked Addresses” > “Create a new filter”.

Specify criteria for the filter (e.g., sender, keywords) and actions (e.g., apply label, archive).

Purpose: Keeps your inbox organized, making it easier to find important emails and manage correspondence.

Using Templates for Common Responses

Steps:

Enable templates by going to Settings > “Advanced” > Enable “Templates”.

Create a template by composing an email, clicking on the three dots (More options) at the bottom, selecting “Templates” > “Save draft as template” > “Save as new template”.

Purpose: Saves time by allowing you to quickly respond to common inquiries with pre-written responses.

Scheduling Emails

Steps:

Compose an email.

Click the drop-down arrow next to the “Send” button and select “Schedule send”.

Choose a date and time to send the email.

Purpose: Ensures emails are sent at the most appropriate time, even if you’re unavailable.

Using Google Chat

Accessing Google Chat

Steps:

Access Google Chat from Gmail or directly at Google Chat.

Sign in with your Google account.

Purpose: Provides an integrated platform for instant messaging within the Google ecosystem.

Starting a Direct Message

Steps:

Click on the “+” icon next to “Chat” to start a new conversation.

Enter the name or email of the person you want to message.

Type your message and press Enter to send.

Purpose: Facilitates quick, informal communication with colleagues or students.

Creating Chat Rooms for Group Discussions

Steps:

Click on the “+” icon next to “Rooms” to create a new chat room.

Name the room and add participants by entering their email addresses.

Use the room for group discussions and collaboration.

Purpose: Supports collaboration by providing a dedicated space for group conversations.

Using Bots and Integrations

Steps:

Click on the “+” icon next to “Chat” and select “Find a bot”.

Browse available bots and add them to your chat.

Use bots for tasks like scheduling meetings, managing tasks, and more.

Purpose: Enhances functionality by automating routine tasks and integrating with other services.

Tips for Success

  • Regular Maintenance: Regularly clean up your inbox and chat rooms to keep them organized.
  • Effective Use of Labels: Use labels and filters to keep track of important emails and avoid clutter.
  • Active Participation: Encourage students and colleagues to actively use Google Chat for quick communication and collaboration.
